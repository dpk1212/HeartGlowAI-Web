Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about ConnectlyAI and how it can help your relationships.

How does ConnectlyAI actually work?

ConnectlyAI uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze your relationship dynamics and create personalized action plans. You'll receive daily steps, conversation prompts, and insights tailored to your specific relationship. Our AI learns from your progress to continuously improve recommendations.

What types of relationships does ConnectlyAI support?

ConnectlyAI supports three main relationship types: romantic relationships (dating, engaged, married), friendships (new and long-term), and family relationships (siblings, parent-child, extended family). Each relationship type receives customized guidance.

Is my relationship data private and secure?

Absolutely. Privacy is our top priority. All your data is encrypted, securely stored, and never shared with third parties. You control what information you share, and you can delete your data at any time. Our privacy policy details how we protect your information.

Do both people in the relationship need to use ConnectlyAI?

No, ConnectlyAI is designed to be effective even if only one person is using it. While having both people engaged can enhance results, you can still improve your relationship by implementing the suggested steps on your own.

When will ConnectlyAI launch?
